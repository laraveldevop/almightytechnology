Almighty Technology

Almighty Technology is an IT solutions provider located in Surat, Gujarat, India.

Founded in 2013, the company specializes in a wide range of services, including desktop and laptop repairs, CCTV camera installations, data recovery, NAS storage, server solutions, virus and malware protection, and networking services.

They also offer annual maintenance contracts (AMC) and IT consulting for both individuals and businesses.
